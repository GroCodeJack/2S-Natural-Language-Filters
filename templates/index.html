Introducing AI Golf Club Search (Beta)

AI Golf Club Search is the fastest way to find exactly what you're looking for. Simply enter your search in natural language—like "Ping right-handed stiff flex drivers under $300"—and our AI will return matching results in seconds.

  • Select club type first: Choose the club type from the dropdown near the search bar before searching
  • Shaft flex filtering: Currently, we can filter by shaft flex options but not specific shaft models
  • Beta version: This feature is in beta and results may not always be perfect—we're continuously improving!
